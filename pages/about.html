About Code_d_Code

Discover the story behind the official coding society of SoET, Vikram University. Founded in 2024, we've grown to 100+ active members united by our passion for technology, innovation, and creating the future through code.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To cultivate a strong coding culture at SoET, Vikram University by providing comprehensive learning opportunities, fostering collaboration among students, and creating innovative solutions that benefit both the academic community and society at large.

We are committed to bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application through hands-on projects, mentorship programs, and industry-relevant skill development initiatives.

Our Vision

To transform students into capable developers and innovators who can contribute meaningfully to the tech industry and society. We aspire to be the premier platform for coding excellence and technological innovation at Vikram University.

We envision a future where Code_d_Code graduates become leaders in technology, driving digital transformation and inspiring the next generation of developers through innovation and excellence.

Our Journey

From inception in 2024 to becoming SoET's leading coding community with 100+ active members

2024

Foundation & Recognition

Code_d_Code was officially established as the coding society of SoET, Vikram University, with founder Sonu Kumar leading the initiative to create a comprehensive platform for coding excellence and innovation.

2024

Campus Vault Launch

Launched our flagship project Campus Vault - a comprehensive digital ecosystem that revolutionizes campus life, establishing Code_d_Code as a leading innovation hub at SoET.

2024

Community Expansion

Rapidly grew to 100+ active members, establishing strong executive leadership with dedicated departments for technical development, events, and community engagement.

2024

Project Portfolio Development

Developed and launched 5+ live projects including our flagship Campus Vault platform, showcasing the technical excellence and innovation capacity of our community.

2024

Official Recognition

Gained official recognition as the premier coding society of SoET, Vikram University, establishing our position as the leading platform for technological innovation and coding excellence on campus.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our community and drive our success

Innovation

We embrace creativity and encourage out-of-the-box thinking to solve complex problems and develop cutting-edge solutions.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of teamwork and create an environment where diverse minds come together to achieve common goals.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in everything we do, from code quality to event organization and community building.

Inclusivity

We welcome everyone regardless of their background or skill level, fostering an inclusive environment where all members can thrive.

Learning

We promote continuous learning and skill development, providing resources and opportunities for personal and professional growth.

Integrity

We maintain the highest ethical standards in all our activities and build trust through transparency and honest communication.
