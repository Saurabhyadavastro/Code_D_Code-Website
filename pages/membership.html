Community Guidelines

Be Respectful

Treat all members with respect and kindness. We welcome people from all backgrounds and skill levels.

Help Others

Share your knowledge and help fellow members. If you ask for help, be prepared to help others too.

Stay On Topic

Keep discussions relevant to programming, technology, and our community activities.

No Spam or Self-Promotion

Avoid excessive self-promotion or spam. Share resources that benefit the community.

Keep Learning

Stay curious and continue learning. Participate actively in our courses and workshops.
