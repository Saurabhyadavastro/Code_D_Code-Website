üåê About Code_d_Code

Code_d_Code is a student-led technical society under the School of Engineering and Technology (SoET), Vikram University, Ujjain. Founded with a vision to promote technical excellence, peer-to-peer learning, and project-based growth among engineering students.

Initiated as an idea in 2023 and officially launched in 2024, Code_d_Code has quickly become one of the most active and engaging student societies within the university.
